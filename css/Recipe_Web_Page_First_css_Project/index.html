Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

For the dumplings:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons lukewarm milk
  • 1 tablespoon yogurt or lemon juice

For the syrup:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon rose water
  • 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions:

Combine milk powder, flour, baking soda, salt, oil, lukewarm milk, and yogurt/le mon juice to form dough. Shape into small balls and deep-fry until golden. Prepare syrup by boiling sugar, water, rose water, cardamom, and lemon juice. Soak fried dumplings in the sweet syrup until well-coated.
